Care Plan Type 2 Diabetes

PREVENTIVE ACTIVITY: IMMUNIZATIONS

  • Follow PIQ for Anti-tetanus
  • Follow PIQ for pneumococcal vaccination
  • Follow PIQ for Zoster-SU vaccine ( >50 yrs)
  • Follow PIQ for HPV vaccine ( women between 18-45 yrs and men 9-26 yrs)


PREVENTIVE ACTIVITY: CANCER

  • Q2 y Mammography ( 50-69 yrs)
  • Q3 y Pap test (21-65 yrs)
  • Q2 y Fit Test (50-74 yrs) if no colonoscopy in the past 10 yrs.


PREVENTIVE ACTIVITY: METABOLIC DISEASES

  • Q1 y Monofilament and foot exam
  • Q2 y Diabetic retinopathy screening
  • Q5 y ECG (If cardiac disease: EKG q 1y)


LABORATORY:

  • Q 3 to 6 mo HbA1C
  • Q1 y Creat, Electrolytes, AcRatio
  • Q3 y Lipid
